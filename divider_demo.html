Section Divider Patterns

A single-page gallery of common built-in shape divider patterns, each rendered with a reusable inline SVG symbol and applied via <use>. Scroll down to see all fourteen patterns in action.

Book

Two symmetric curves meet at a center point, like an open book. Great for a calm, formal transition between a hero and body content.
